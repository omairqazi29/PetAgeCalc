About the App

Pet Age Calc helps you understand your pet's age in human years using scientifically-based calculations. Whether you have a dog, cat, rabbit, hamster, guinea pig, or parrot, our app provides accurate age conversions based on species and size.

How to Use

  1. Select your pet type from the dropdown menu
  2. Enter your pet's age in years
  3. Tap "Calculate Human Age"
  4. View the equivalent human age and life stage description

Frequently Asked Questions

Q: How accurate are the age calculations?

A: Our calculations are based on commonly accepted veterinary guidelines and scientific research. Different species and sizes age at different rates, which our app takes into account.

Q: Which pets are supported?

A: We support dogs (small, medium, large), cats, rabbits, hamsters, guinea pigs, and parrots.

Q: Does the app collect any data?

A: No! All calculations are performed locally on your device. We don't collect, store, or transmit any user data. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Q: Is the app free?

A: Yes, Pet Age Calc is completely free with no ads or in-app purchases.

Q: Can I enter decimal ages?

A: Yes! You can enter ages like "2.5" for more precise calculations.
